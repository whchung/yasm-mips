char yasm_valparam::id_prefix

Prefix character that indicates a raw identifier. When yasm_vp_string() is called on a YASM_PARAM_ID, all characters are returned. When yasm_vp_id() is called on a YASM_PARAM_ID, if the identifier begins with this character, this character is stripped from the returned value.
