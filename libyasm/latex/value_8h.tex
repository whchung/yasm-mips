Break a \hyperlink{structyasm__expr}{yasm\-\_\-expr} into a \hyperlink{structyasm__value}{yasm\-\_\-value} constituent parts. Extracts the relative portion of the value, S\-E\-G and W\-R\-T portions, and top-\/level right shift, if any. Places the remaining expr into the absolute portion of the value. Essentially a combination of \hyperlink{value_8h_ac0d50820e1b7485957ea3985c4bb64d3}{yasm\-\_\-value\-\_\-initialize()} and \hyperlink{value_8h_a8f5f80b3c9fabd162683998fb5c6785a}{yasm\-\_\-value\-\_\-finalize()}. First expands references to symrecs in absolute sections by expanding with the absolute section start plus the symrec offset within the absolute section.
